Hi there, My name is Sophia Xueying Li. 🙂

Sophia earned an Honours BSc degree in Pharmacology and Biomedical Toxicology from University of Toronto with a passion in health informatics and health research. She is currently pursuing a master's degree in Health Informatics at the Institute of Health Policy, Management and Evaluation, Dalla Lana School of Public Health at UofT. The MHI program is an AI-related master’s program recognized by The Vector Institute.

Her current professional experience involves in working as a Research Assistant at the Centre for Addiction and Mental Health building and validating various statistical models to evaluate study treatments. With her training in Health Informatics, she has developed a passion in advanced data analytics to provide data-driven recommendations and solutions to improve healthcare.

Besides academics and work, she has been mentoring youth for many years. She is also heavily involved in community volunteering with a leadership role.
